About

I am a web developer based in Ljubljana, Slovenia, with over 5 years of commercial experience. I specialise in building accessible websites using Zope, Plone, Grok, repoze.bfg. I am available as an external contractor for web development through to training and consultancy, on a negotiable basis. For more information, please don’t hesitate to get in contact.

Philosophy

My moto is ‘get paid to do things for free’. Not to get to philosofical about it, i beleive in open source philosophy and work in the spirit of it. Earning a living by it is simply my dream.
